Mentors


John Jameson




John is a pottery instuctor. He has been working with clay and pottery for over 30 years working with all kinds of events. He is currently the owner of the creative center and has been running it for 10 years. John has his master potter certificate as of 2001.


Ryan Reynolds




Ryan began practicing yoga in 2001. He has recieived his 300 hour yoga Teacher certificate in 2009. He has a Ashtanga teaching style. He emphasizes in breathing excersizes and physical form. This is a very physical, flow style yoga.


Chelsea Smalls




Chelsea is a newly certified glass blowing teacher. She has been glass blowing since 2005 with over 600 hours in it. She is a very hard working and great mentor that will get you to try glass blowing or get you to the next level.


Bobert Robert




Bobert is our painting instructor. Bobert has been has earned a BA in Arts. He focuses on many different classes that focus on painting. He has a great passion for watching young adults grow in their passions and skills for painting.


Stephanie Michaels




Stephanie is a master woodworker. She owned her own woodworking business for 24 years before retiring from there. She has been teaching at the local college Ford Community College as a part time job. In her limited time after that she is a woodworking mentor with us.
